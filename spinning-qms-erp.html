Formula reference — ISO 11462-1 validated
Ne (hank count) = (L × 0.54) / W_grams where L = sample length (yards) W = weight (grams) Derivation: Ne = L / (840 × W_lbs) = L × 453.592 / (840 × W_g) = L × 0.5400 / W_g ✓
CV% = (σ / x̄) × 100 σ = √[Σ(xᵢ-x̄)²/(n-1)] [sample] Cpk = min[(USL-x̄)/3σ, (x̄-LSL)/3σ] Cp = (USL-LSL) / 6σ UCL = x̄ + 3σ/√n LCL = x̄ - 3σ/√n WL± = x̄ ± 2σ/√n

Operator quick-reference guide
1. Hank count formula — verified (ISO 11462-1)
Ne = (Sample length in yards × 0.54) / Weight in grams Example: 6 yards, 26.94 g → (6 × 0.54) / 26.94 = 3.24 / 26.94 = 0.1203 Ne Source: Ne = L(yds)/(840 × W(lbs)) = L × 453.592/(840 × W_g) = L × 0.54/W_g
2. Department targets (hardcoded)
Carding: 0.120 Ne Breaker/RSB: 0.120 Ne RSB: 0.120 Ne Simplex: 1.120 Ne Ring Frame: 47.5 Ne ±0.5 (tolerance: 46.5–47.5) Autoconer: 47.0 Ne ±0.5 (tolerance: 46.5–47.5)
3. Western Electric Control Rules
Rule 1: 1 point beyond 3σ action limit → STOP machine immediately Rule 2: 2 of 3 consecutive beyond 2σ warning limit (same side) → Check machine Rule 3: 4 of 5 consecutive beyond 1σ (same side) → Trend detected, monitor closely Rule 4: 8 consecutive points same side of centerline → Systematic drift, adjust draft ratio
4. Interpreting CV%
CV% ≤ Uster 25th — top quartile, excellent CV% 25th–50th — acceptable, monitor CV% > Uster 75th — action required
5. Machine adjustment suggestions
AlertAction
1 point beyond 3σStop machine. Check draft settings, sliver can, gear teeth. Recalibrate rollers.
2 of 3 beyond 2σCheck drafting zone, aprons, top rollers. Inspect clothing, calendar conditions. Take 2 extra samples.
Systematic drift (8 pts)Check draft ratio, temperature, humidity. Verify belt tension, bearing lubrication.
CV% > Uster 75thCheck card clothing, flats, calendar rollers. Inspect fibre quality. Inform QC supervisor.
Cpk < 1.33Process is not capable. Review specification limits and draft ratio optimization.
6. Index of Irregularity (I)
I = CV_actual / CV_theoretical I < 1.1 → Excellent (near ideal random drafting) I 1.1–1.3 → Acceptable I 1.3–1.5 → High — check drafting system I > 1.5 → Critical — systematic machine fault
